Pokellector Set Downloader

Use this extension to download set lists from Pokellector.com in CSV format for your own unique use in a spreadsheet or anything else.

In order for the extension to work you must be on a "Set" page that shows the checklist and text. with list_display=list at the end.

Example Page that works: https://www.pokellector.com/sets/BS-Base-Set?list_display=list

Note - This extension is not affiliated with Pokellector or the Pokemon company in any way shape or form. I am just a hobbyist who wanted to organize the sets I'm collecting in different orders than is provided by Pokellector.com
