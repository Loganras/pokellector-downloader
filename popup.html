Pokellector Set Downloader

Instructions

  1. In your browser, navigate to Pokellector.com
  2. Navigate to the set you want to download
  3. Click the checklist view for your set on Pokellector.com
  4. Check your browser url for this at the end: ?list_display=list
  5. Click the Download Set button
  6. A CSV file should download to your device

About

Use this extension to download set lists from Pokellector.com in CSV format for your own unique use in a spreadsheet or anything else.

In order for the extension to work you must be on a "Set" page that shows the checklist and text. See Instructions above.

Example Page that works (As of April 24 2021): https://www.pokellector.com/sets/BS-Base-Set?list_display=list

Caveats

This is an unofficial extension, if Pokellector.com updates their website, there's a good chance this will stop working until the extension also gets an update.

Note - This extension is not affiliated with Pokellector or the Pokemon company in any way. I am just a hobbyist who wanted a way to organize the sets I'm collecting.
